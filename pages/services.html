Budgeting Made Easy

Weddings can be expensive and with so much going on, it is all too easy to lose track of what you are spending.

Ready Confetti has an easy-to-use budget planner, which allows you to set a budget and track in real-time what you are spending. You can schedule when payments are due and our planner will create a cash flow that tells you how much money you will need at each stage of your wedding journey.
